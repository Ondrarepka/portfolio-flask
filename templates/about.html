About Me

Welcome to my portfolio website! Here you'll find information about my projects, skills, and experiences.

Short bio

My name is Ondřej Řepka. I was born in Mladá Boleslav, Czech Republic. I am designer by heart, but I always try to be the renaissance man in the digital age. So I can do a bit of coding (like this portfolio, or other things you can find in my projects page), a bit of graphics design, and a bit of writing.

I studied Information science with main focus put to Service and Interface design and Ethics at Masaryk University in Brno, where I am currently located.

Skills

  • Iterative Design & User Research (I adore user interviews and usability testing, but can also handle more quantitative methods)
  • Graphic Design: Figma
  • Writing: My strongest area is User-facing documentation and Technical writing
  • Other: Linux system administration, Basic knowledge of Python and SQL
  • Web Development: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Flask

Interests

In my free time, I enjoy playing tabletop role-playing games (TTRPGs) such as Vampire the Masquerade, FATE, or czech original Dračí Doupě. Most of the time I am the storyteller, because I love world-building and creating immersive experiences for players to explore.

As most of guys my age, I love to play some video games from time to time, focusing on the ones with steeper learning curves, like Escape from Tarkov, Hunt: Showdown or ARC Raiders. And recently I picked up an old childhood hobby of mine, I play Magic the Gathering.

I also love to play 12-string guitar and sing.

If you'd like to get in touch or learn more about my work, please don't hesitate to reach out!
