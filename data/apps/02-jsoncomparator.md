## Solution to my work problems
Does your app run on JSON configs too? Do you have to push them into PROD? Then you already know the pain — hunting down the differences, because you really don't want TEST environment leftovers sneaking into PROD, but you also can't afford to miss something that actually matters.

That was exactly my itch, so I built a little utility for myself, because Total Commander's compare just wasn't cutting it anymore.
